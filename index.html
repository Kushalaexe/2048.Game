2048

HOW TO PLAY?

  • Swipe to move tiles and combine them

  • Use the arrow keys to move the tiles in four directions: left ⬅️, right ➡️, up ⬆️, and down ⬇️ (For Laptop users).

  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with the sum of the numbers.

  • A new tile with a 2 appears randomly in an empty spot after every move.

  • The goal is to create a tile with the number 2048.


  • NOTE : When you click on restart button, score will automatically reset once you moved tiles
